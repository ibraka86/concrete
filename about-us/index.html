About Us

Simplifying masonry estimation for pros and homeowners alike.

Concrete Block Calculator was born from a simple need: a fast, accurate, and accessible way to estimate masonry materials without complex spreadsheets or manual math.

Our Mission

We aim to provide the construction industry and DIY community with high-quality estimation tools that save time and reduce material waste. Our calculators are developed based on industry-standard formulas and real-world construction practices.

Our Tools

  • CMU Calculator: The ultimate tool for standard block walls, grout, and reinforcement.
  • Retaining Wall Estimator: Specialized for stacked and structural retaining walls.
  • Block Weight Tool: Crucial for logistics and structural load planning.
  • Fire Pit & Planter Tool: Perfect for planning weekend DIY projects.
