Retaining Wall Formula: Proper Construction and Structural Security

The Retaining Wall Block Counting Formula

To calculate the number of blocks required: (Wall Length × Wall Height) ÷ (Block Face Area). Our calculator to estimate materials also rounds up for waste to ensure you don't run short during construction.

Gravel and Base Material Calculation

A proper foundation requires gravel. Our gravel calculator logic suggests a depth of 6-8 inches per row. We recommend using landscape fabric and a drainage system to protect the structural integrity of your retaining wall project.

Gravity Walls & Structural Security

A gravity wall uses its own concrete block weight to maintain security. For walls higher than 4 feet, we recommend a reinforced design with geogrid to prevent soil failure.
